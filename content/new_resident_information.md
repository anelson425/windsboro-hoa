Welcome to Windsboro! We're glad you're here. This page has everything you need to get settled into the community.

### Get Started with PayHOA

All HOA activity is managed through our {{< payhoa_link "PayHOA portal" >}}. Once registered, you can:

* Pay your annual HOA dues
* Request a pool access card
* Submit architectural change requests
* View community announcements and documents

### Know the Rules

Before making any changes to the exterior of your home — including fencing, landscaping, sheds, or paint color — review the [HOA Documents](/documents) page and submit an architectural change request through PayHOA for approval.

### Stay Connected

Check the [Calendar](/calendar) for upcoming HOA meetings and community events. To reach the board or a committee member, visit the [Contacts](/contacts) page.

We encourage all new residents to attend the annual homeowners meeting — it's a great way to meet neighbors and get involved in the community. If you have any questions not answered here, don't hesitate to reach out to the general inbox.
