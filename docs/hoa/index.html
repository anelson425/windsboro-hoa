Windsboro HOA Bylaws and Amended Covenants and Restrictions

Windsboro HOA Bylaws along with our covenants and restrictions protect each homeowner and help our community to maintain the lifestyle we all were seeking when purchasing our homes here in Windsboro. Each homeowner has a voice in our community. We encourage everyone to attend our annual homeowner's meetings and volunteer for one of our Committees or to run for an HOA Board Member position.

Architectural Committee Review Form

Are you planning to update or make external improvements to your Windsboro home? Please make sure you have submitted a copy of the Request for Architectural Committee Approval form along with your proposal of this work to Rob Gannon Committee Chair. The Architectural Committee will review your proposal for the alterations to your property in accordance with existing topography, relation to surrounding structures and general harmony of existing appearance.

HOA Board Meetings
